# The 9 Dimensions of Super-Resilience by Alan Heeks

The mention of more severe weather in this article stands out to me, since there was recently a big snow storm. And hearing how more extreme weather can become increasingly common makes me realize how important it is to prepare something like a go bag. Even though I would want to prepare beyond that. Also, I like seeing the things I can do for my physical health. It is something I take very seriously, and it is important the unexpected can impact physical health significantly. I became aware of stress induced illnesses very recently. But I feel like I’ve been cursed with knowledge because now I’m gonna stress about how I need to avoid stress.

# What Resilience is Not by Nikki Andersen

Certain headers in the article stand out to me as I see them as applicable to me. Specifically the ones about independence and hardening up. I know I’m an independent person and have moments where I feel the need to harden up to keep going, and it is helpful to see this article tell me that resilience is not those things. Not that I had previously thought I was resilient because of them, but still. Nice to see what isn’t resilience.
