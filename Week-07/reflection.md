# Reflection

Think of a recipe, flavor, or ingredient that is meaningful to you. Reflect on where it comes from, who it connects you to, and why it matters in your life. What memories, places, or relationships does it carry? What does it reveal about care, culture, or belonging.

A meaningful ingredient to me is basil. I would smell it a lot growing up because it smells so good. It is also important for a dish that I really like. It is called tallarin verda. When I visit family in Ecuador, this is one of the dishes made for me.
